WHAT DO WE DO?

We are creating spaces that bring people together and enjoy food. For each of our events, we’ll provide the food, and you just bring yourself. You’ll meet some new people you might not normally meet, and to enjoy some eats as well.

WHY DO WE DO THIS?

We are a group of friends who love bringing people together through food. Sitting down and sharing food is one of the most fundamental human acts. Yet, in a world where everyone is constantly going somewhere or doing something, it’s often hard to take the time to simply sit with people and enjoy some great food.
