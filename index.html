SUPPER CLUB

We want to bring back the traditional supper club, but with a twist. At Supper Club, we’ll craft a 3-course menu and gather 6-7 strangers to share it. It’s intimate, and may start out a little awkward, but by the end you’ll be laughing and talking as if you’d always been friends. This summer, sign up to hear about Supper Club in San Francisco.

HORS D'OEUVRES

Hors d’Oeuvres are a beautiful way to sample a variety of interesting flavors and dishes and relax after a long day. We’ll gather 8-10 people to share some appetizers while having great conversation and enjoying each other’s company. The catch is that everyone has to bring something. It could be a story, an instrument, a drink, literally anything. Sign up to hear about Hors d’Oeuvres in New York City, Philadelphia, San Francisco, and South Bay (NorCal).

BREAKING BREAD

If you look at regional cuisines around the globe, one of the only constants is bread. Historically, bread has always been the staple of the meal. Moreover, fresh baked bread just tastes amazing. At Breaking Bread, you’ll enjoy some freshly baked bread with wine and cheese pairings while relaxing and enjoying some great conversation with some random people. Sign up to hear about Breaking Bread in South Bay (NorCal).

WHAT?

Food with Friends is a space that brings people together to enjoy food and each other’s company. We’ll cook the food, you just bring yourself. Each experience is uniquely crafted by the people that attend and the food served.

WHY?

We are a group of friends who love cooking and are fascinated by food’s ability to bring people together. We started doing this at our university, and we wanted to continue this summer when we went to different cities across the country to work. We believe that taking the time to sit down and share food is one of the most fundamental human acts, yet is unfortunately rare in these fast-paced times.
