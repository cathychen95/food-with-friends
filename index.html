SUPPER CLUB

We want to bring back the supper club. At supper club, we’ll craft a 3-course menu and gather 6-7 people to share it. It’s intimate, and quite frankly, may be a bit awkward in the beginning, but by the end you’ll be laughing and talking as if you’d always been friends.

HORS D'OEUVRES

Hors d’Oeuvres are a beautiful way to sample a variety of interesting flavors and dishes. We’ll gather 8-9 people and we’ll share some appetizers while having great conversation and enjoying each other’s company. The catch is that everyone has to bring something. It could be a story, an instrument, a drink, literally anything.

BREAKING BREAD

Bread is one of the most fundamental staples of any culture’s cuisine. Moreover, fresh baked bread just tastes amazing. At Breaking Bread, you’ll enjoy some freshly baked bread with wine and cheese pairings while relaxing and enjoying some great conversation with some random people.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We are creating spaces that bring people together and enjoy food. For each of our events, we’ll provide the food, and you just bring yourself. You’ll meet some new people you might not normally meet, and to enjoy some eats as well.

WHY DO WE DO THIS?

We are a group of friends who love bringing people together through food. Sitting down and sharing food is one of the most fundamental human acts. Yet, in a world where everyone is constantly going somewhere or doing something, it’s often hard to take the time to simply sit with people and enjoy some great food.
